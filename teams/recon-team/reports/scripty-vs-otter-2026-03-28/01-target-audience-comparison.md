# Report 1: Target Audience Comparison
**Agent:** Web Researcher + Market Researcher
**Date:** 2026-03-28

---

## Otter.ai's Target Audience

| Segment | Priority | Use Case | Key Need |
|---------|----------|----------|----------|
| **Sales Teams** | Primary | Live meeting notes, CRM sync, deal intelligence | Real-time transcription during Zoom/Meet/Teams |
| **Enterprise** | Primary | Compliance, governance, team-wide meeting knowledge | Security, SSO, admin controls |
| **Education** | Secondary | Lecture capture (student-facing) | Free/cheap, note organization |
| **Media/Content** | Secondary | Interview transcription, podcast notes | Accuracy, speaker ID |
| **Individual Professionals** | Tertiary | Personal meeting productivity | Quick summaries, action items |

**Otter's DNA:** It's a **live meeting tool**. Everything flows from real-time capture during Zoom/Teams/Meet calls. The bot joins your meeting, transcribes live, and generates summaries in real-time. Post-meeting is secondary.

**Pricing signal:** Free tier is unlimited meetings (basic). Paid ($19.99/mo) is for teams who need collaboration + CRM integration. Their money comes from **enterprise sales teams**.

## Scripty's Target Audience

| Segment | Priority | Use Case | Key Need |
|---------|----------|----------|----------|
| **Students** | Primary | Lecture recording transcription, exam prep, study guides | Affordable, accurate, multi-language |
| **Hebrew/Arabic Speakers** | Primary | RTL language transcription + translation | Multi-provider for non-English accuracy |
| **Researchers/Academics** | Secondary | Interview/focus group transcription | Long-form accuracy, export formats |
| **Content Creators** | Secondary | Podcast/video transcription, subtitles | VTT output, translation |
| **Freelancers** | Tertiary | Client meeting notes, translation | Pay-per-use flexibility |

**Scripty's DNA:** It's a **file-based transcription + translation tool** with an academic focus. Users upload recordings after the fact. The value is in the processing pipeline (multi-provider accuracy, cost optimization, translation) and the learning features being built.

---

## The Critical Difference

| Dimension | Otter.ai | Scripty |
|-----------|----------|--------|
| **When** | Real-time (during meeting) | Post-recording (after capture) |
| **Where** | Inside Zoom/Meet/Teams | Standalone upload |
| **Who pays** | Company (per-seat SaaS) | Individual (credit-based) |
| **Core language** | English (primarily) | Multi-language (100+ via GCP, Hebrew/Arabic strength) |
| **Revenue model** | Per-user subscription | Credit-based pay-per-use |
| **Platform** | Meeting assistant bot | Transcription + learning engine |

**Key insight:** These are NOT the same product. Otter is a meeting productivity tool that happens to transcribe. Scripty is a transcription engine that's evolving into a learning platform. The overlap is only in "transcription" — everything else diverges.
